🔥 TODAY'S SESSION AT A GLANCE
🎬 Hook (30 sec) — grab attention immediately
👤 Job Seekers (5-7 min) — openings + energy
🔄 Transition (30 sec) — switch gears smoothly
🏢 Employers (3-5 min) — the pitch
🎯 Closing (1-2 min) — strong recap + CTA
🎬 THE HOOK — First 30 Seconds

💡 Energy here decides if people stay. Smile while you talk — it comes through your voice!

"Heeeey what's up everyone! 👋 Welcome, welcome — thanks so much for tapping in! Quick intro — I'm Kycee from SevenHires Uganda, and today's session is a GOOD one. Whether you're out here job hunting, or you're a business owner who's tired of struggling to find good staff... stay right here, because I've got something for both of you. Don't scroll past — give me 5 minutes and let's see who's getting matched today! 🔥"
👤 SEGMENT 1: Job Seekers — "Let's Talk Jobs!"

💡 Build excitement before listing roles — make it feel like a reveal!

"Okay so let's get into it — I've got a list of REAL openings, with REAL companies, looking for REAL people, RIGHT NOW. These are not random — every single one of these is verified by us. So let's go! ✅ [Go through each opening like this:] 'First up — [Job Title] at [Company/Industry], based in [Area]. They're looking for someone with [1-2 key requirements]. If that's you... 👀 you might want to grab a pen!' [Repeat for 3-5 roles, keep energy up between each one — pause, react: 'ooh this one's a good one' / 'this one's competitive so don't sleep on it'] So how do you apply? Super simple — WhatsApp me right now on [YOUR NUMBER], send your CV, and tell me which role caught your eye. Mention you joined from this session so I know to prioritize you! 🙌"
💬 Engagement Boosters (Use Between Roles)

💡 Drop these in naturally to keep listeners interacting — comments/shares = more reach!

"If you're still listening, drop a 🔥 in the comments so I know you're locked in!" "Tag someone who's been job hunting — this could literally be their day!" "Don't worry if none of these fit YOU — share this session, because it might be exactly what your cousin or friend needs." "I see some of you joining late — don't worry, I'll recap everything at the end, so stick around!"
🔄 Transition — Switch Gears
"Alright, alright — now let's switch it up a bit. 🔄 This next part is for a different crowd — if you're a business owner, you run a restaurant, a clinic, a shop, an office, ANYTHING — stay tuned, because what I'm about to share could save you so much time and stress."
🏢 SEGMENT 2: Employers — "Let's Talk Business!"

💡 Lead with their pain point — make them nod along before you pitch.

"Let me ask you something — have you ever needed to hire someone, and just... ugh. CVs everywhere, half the people don't even show up for interviews, and weeks go by with the position still empty? 😩 Yeah. We fix that. I'm Kycee from SevenHires Uganda — we connect businesses with vetted, ready-to-work candidates. Receptionists, waitstaff, drivers, accountants, nurses, cleaners, security, sales reps — literally any role you can think of, we've got people ready. Here's the best part — registering your business with us is 100% FREE. You tell us what kind of roles you usually need, and the moment a vacancy opens — even last minute — we send you candidates who are ALREADY screened and ready to interview. No stress, no waiting weeks. And here's a pro tip — even if you're not hiring TODAY, register anyway! Why? Because the day someone resigns out of nowhere (and trust me, it happens 😅), you won't be starting from zero. We'll already have you in our system, ready to go. So — interested? Two ways: comment 'REGISTER' right now and I'll personally reach out to you after this session, OR WhatsApp me directly on [YOUR NUMBER]. Either way, it takes less than 5 minutes and there's ZERO obligation. 🙌"
🎯 THE CLOSING — Strong Finish

💡 Recap with energy — this is often the most-remembered part!

"Okay guys, that's it for today — but before you go, let's do a quick recap because I know some of you joined late! 🎬 For job seekers — we've got openings for [quickly name 2-3 standout roles] — WhatsApp me on [YOUR NUMBER] with your CV and the role you want. For business owners — comment 'REGISTER' or WhatsApp me, and let's get your business set up with reliable staff, completely free. Huge thank you to everyone who stayed, commented, shared — you guys are amazing! 🙏 Follow SevenHires Uganda so you don't miss the next session — we do these regularly and you NEVER know who might get matched next time. Alright, that's a wrap — see you all next time! Bye for now! 👋✨"
✅ DOs — Keep It Engaging
• Smile while talking — energy is audible! • Use pauses and reactions ("ooh this one's good!") — feels live, not scripted • Repeat your WhatsApp number often — people join mid-session • Ask for comments/reactions — boosts visibility AND keeps people present • Keep each job short and punchy — title, location, 1 requirement • Tell people you'll recap at the end — keeps late-joiners listening • Use real (anonymized) success stories if you have them • Vary your tone — excited for good roles, warm for the employer pitch • End with a CLEAR next step for both audiences
❌ DON'Ts
• Don't promise a job — say "opportunity," never guarantee • Don't give out direct client contact details — go through YOU/SevenHires • Don't share salary figures unless confirmed — say "competitive salary" • Don't quote exact pricing/fees unless confirmed • Don't promise specific timeframes unless confirmed • Don't name-drop client businesses without permission • Don't badmouth other agencies • Don't make up job details — only share confirmed info • Don't speak in monotone — flat energy = people leave
📋 Have Ready Before Going Live
For each job opening: • Job title • Industry/Company type (anonymize if needed) • Location/Area • 1-2 key requirements • How to apply (your WhatsApp/phone/email) • Application deadline if any General: • Your WhatsApp number (repeat often!) • 3-5 current job openings (pick your BEST ones first) • Keyword for employers ("REGISTER") • Keyword for job seekers ("JOBS" or role name) • A glass of water 😄 — you'll be talking a lot!
